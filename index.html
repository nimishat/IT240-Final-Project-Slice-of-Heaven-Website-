At Slice of Heaven we pride ourselves on customer service. Over the last 5 years we have been serving the Boston area with our coal fire pizza. We also offer a wide selection of beer, wine, and spirits to fit customer needs. Our team came together while attending courses at the University of Massachusetts Boston. With all the great cuisine around the Boston area it was very difficult to find a good slice of pie. The collaborative effect of Nimisha Thakkar, Matthew Crenshaw, and Yvenord Jean has allowed our fellow beacon's and neighbors alike a premium option in the pizza game. Come out to one of our four locations and enjoy a slice of heaven.
