Pick your Slice!

Hover over any of the cards to see a picture of the type of pizza and then a description about it on the back!

"Margarita"

Our Margaritta is made with fresh pulled mozzarella, fresh basic, and our house made sauce.

"Meat Lovers"

The Meat lover is beautiful medly of our house cured meats. A mixture of prosciutto, pepperoni, Salami, and chorizo, that will bring a smile to any carnivore.

"Cheese Classico"

The Classico is perfect for all pizza traditionalist. cheese, sauce and fresh basil.

"Sausage Overload"

The Sausage Overload features 1/2 pound of sausage for a hearty, salty, bite.

"The Beach Bum"

The Beach Bum is a sweet and salty treat. We combine ham, pineapple, and shredded coconut, for a taste of the tropics.

"Buffalo Soldier"

The Buffalo Soldier is a medley of fire spiced chicken, jalapeno peppers, and a buffalo based sauce.

"Brazilian Style"

The Brazilian features green olives, boiled eggs and honey ham.

"Pale Kale"

The Pale Kale is a five cheese medley with an alfredo based sauce topped with fresh kale.

"Celiac Special (Gluten Free)""

The Celiac Special is a gluten free option. Each pie includes two toppings of your choice. (.75 for each additional topping)

"The Corny Boar"

The Corny Boar is a combination of wild boar sausage, fresh corn, and a tangy BBQ sauce.

"Veggie Supreme"

The Veggie Surpreme is a mix of spinach, red peppers, mushrooms, broccoli, and asparagus.

"Woodland"

The Woodland is a mushroom lovers paradise, featuring a mixture of six varieties of wild fungi.
